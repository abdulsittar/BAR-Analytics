Analysis through information propagation network

Information propagation network allows visualizing the information spreading. It refers to the representation of the transmission and dissemination of information in a graphical or visual format. The purpose of visualizing information spreading is to help better understand and analyze the patterns, trends, and dynamics of how information travels across different barriers. This type of visualization can be useful in various fields, such as communication, social network analysis, digital humanities, journalism and epidemiology.
